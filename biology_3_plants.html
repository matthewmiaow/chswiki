So spoke Gandalf the wise.

Here you can find links to online resources expanding on some of the key concepts in this standard

You'll need to describe various processes within each response of a plant or animal to their external environment, and then explain the selective advantage (i.e. how it helps an organism survive and reproduce) of each advantage

Types of responses include:

  • Orientation in space (tropisms, nastic responses, taxes, kineses, homing, migration
  • orientation in time (annual, daily, lunar, tidal rhythmns
  • interspecific relationships (competition for resources, mutualism, exploitation, predation and parasitism)
  • intraspecific relationships (competition for resources, territoriality, hierarchical behaviour, cooperative interactions, reproductive behaviours)
